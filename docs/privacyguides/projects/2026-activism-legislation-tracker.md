Privacy Guides is fundraising for the development and ongoing maintenance of a tool to track privacy legislation and policy developments around the world, with information on how to get involved with them. This will be a resource for activists, journalists, and anyone interested in data privacy to find and support relevant legislation in their country or region.

This directory will be a part of our larger activism section for privacy advocacy, which includes resources like the Privacy Activism Toolkit. We believe that empowering individuals with the knowledge and tools to advocate for their privacy rights is crucial in the fight against surveillance and data exploitation.

If you want to make these tools a reality, please consider donating to support our work! The activism section is a long-term project that will require significant resources to develop and maintain, and your support can help us make it a success.

Contributions towards this goal are non-refundable donations to the non-profit *Privacy Guides* program fiscally hosted by MAGIC Grants (a 501(c)(3) organization), not a purchase of a product or service.
