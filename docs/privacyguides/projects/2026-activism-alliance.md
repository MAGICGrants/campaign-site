Privacy Guides is fundraising for the development and ongoing maintenance of a resource to share information about local activists and advocates, and organizations working on privacy issues, allowing them to connect, share resources, and collaborate on campaigns and initiatives.

This directory and community will include:

- An events calendar of upcoming privacy-related events, protests, conferences, and webinars around the world.
- A meetup directory of local privacy activism groups and meetups, allowing people to find and connect with others in their area.
- A directory of organizations working on privacy issues, including non-profits, advocacy groups, and research institutions, with information on their work and how to get involved or support them.

For local meetup groups and organizations, we will host tools to help them organize and promote their events, share resources, and connect with other groups.

This directory will be a part of our larger activism section for privacy advocacy, which includes resources like the Privacy Activism Toolkit. We believe that empowering individuals with the knowledge and tools to advocate for their privacy rights is crucial in the fight against surveillance and data exploitation.

If you want to make these tools a reality, please consider donating to support our work! The activism section is a long-term project that will require significant resources to develop and maintain, and your support can help us make it a success.
