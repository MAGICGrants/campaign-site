[*This Week in Privacy*](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL1m__5QdVZYwrrfLgBBAypcKMBlJtXUSP) is a livestream series produced by Privacy Guides that covers the latest news and developments in data privacy, surveillance, and digital rights. The show features analysis and commentary on the most important privacy-related stories of the week, as well as Q&A sessions with viewers, and a showcase section of new initiatives in the privacy space.

This goal represents the operational costs for producing and maintaining the *This Week in Privacy* livestream series in 2026, including research, production, and editing costs for [clips](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCKgzASKTc5n1uobNsf0zn0w/) and other promotion. We believe that providing regular, high-quality content on privacy issues is crucial for raising awareness and empowering individuals to take action to protect their privacy rights.

Our weekly show is primarily funded by general donations and monthly membership support. Reaching this goal earlier in the year will ensure we can continue to produce the show without interruption, and allow us to invest in improving the production quality and reach of the show. If you enjoy our content and want to support our work, please consider donating or becoming a member!

---

Contributions towards this goal are non-refundable donations to the non-profit *Privacy Guides* program fiscally hosted by MAGIC Grants (a 501(c)(3) organization), not a purchase of a product or service. If this goal is not reached, we will do our best to use the funds raised towards this project, towards other video projects, or towards our general funds to support our work.
