Our video production team is primarily funded by general donations and monthly membership support. We have a large queue of videos that we want to produce, and a very small team working to make them happen, so we have to prioritize which videos we produce based on the resources we have available.

This is a lower priority video that we want to produce, but it is a very important topic that we want to cover. This series will be a video adaptation of our [Privacy Activist Toolbox](https://www.privacyguides.org/en/activism/toolbox/), a collection of 25 articles with various tools, techniques, and resources to help privacy activists and advocates fight for digital rights and privacy protections most effectively. We believe that providing high-quality, informative videos on important topics like this is crucial for raising awareness and empowering individuals to take action to protect their privacy rights.

This collection of resources is very extensive, and adapting them to video format will require a significant amount of time and effort, which would set back the production of other videos in our queue.

However, reaching this goal will allow us to prioritize the creation of this series, and ensure that we can produce it with the quality and attention to detail that it deserves.
