Privacy Guides is the top resource for information and tools to protect your privacy online, with a website that receives millions of visitors each year.

We currently have a very generous team of volunteers who translate our content into various languages, but with this goal we would be able to fund professional translation services to complete many of our incomplete or outdated translations, translate to new languages, and make our content accessible to a global audience. This would allow us to reach more people with our resources and tools, and ensure that our content is accurate and high-quality in all languages.

We understand this is certainly a stretch goal, but if you believe that making our content accessible to a global audience is important, please consider donating or becoming a member to help us make this a reality. We will attempt to effectively use donations towards this goal even if we do not reach the full amount, so any support is appreciated!
